About Me

I am an economist with an M.Phil. in Applied Economics from the University of London. My career spans research, teaching, and policy advisory roles, with a focus on economic development, environmental policy, and education reform.

My passion lies in bridging the gap between academic research and real-world policy impact. I aim to promote inclusive economic growth through data-driven insights and evidence-based recommendations.
